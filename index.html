About Chris

Chris Bates (BMus) is an Auckland-based musician and recording artist offering one-to-one lessons in guitar, singing, and songwriting. Lessons are available on Auckland’s North Shore or online — focused on building skill, creativity, and confidence with the perspective of a working artist and a dedicated tutor.
