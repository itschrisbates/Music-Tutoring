Lessons

One-on-one music tutoring on Auckland’s North Shore, for all skill levels. I specialise in vocals, acoustic guitar, and songwriting — always tailored to your goals and the music you love.

Vocals

Personalised vocal coaching focused on building technique and confidence. We’ll work on the fundamentals (breath, placement, control, vocal health) and apply it directly to songs you want to sing.

Acoustic guitar

Practical, song-first lessons. We can cover chords, rhythm, fingerpicking, learning songs efficiently, and (if you want) developing your ear and musical feel.

Songwriting

Write better songs and find your voice. We’ll explore chord progressions, melody, lyrics, structure, and break down great songs to see what makes them work — then apply it to your own writing.

Format

One-on-one lessons in 30, 45, or 60 minute sessions. Based on the North Shore — feel free to ask about location/travel.

Feel free to flick me an email — I’d love to hear about you and your goals.
